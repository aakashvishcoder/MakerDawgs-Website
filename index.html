MNHS TSA ENGINEERING CLUB

Purpose

Makerdawgs is MNHS's engineering & STEM club. Here, we all join together and compete in various competitions and events apart of the Technology Student Organization, for which you can be recognized regionally, at the state-level, or even nationally in various events! However, it is not necessary to compete in order to be a Makerdawg. Yet, it is highly encouraged as there are so many different categories and competitions for everyone!

Mission Statement

Empowering students and cultivating innovation, teamwork, and a passion for technology.
